•WHY PRAIA DA ROCHA?•

Beautiful rock formations and sandy beaches

Praia da Rocha has a grand main beach and right next to it you will find smaller beaches like Praia dos Tres Castelos, Praia da Marina de Portimão and Praia dos Careanos. Praia da Rocha also provides cafès, restaurants, corner stores and ice cream parlours. Not to mention hairdressers, massage studios and other "essential" services.
Fort of Santa Catarina stands proudly at the mouth of Arade River facing Atlantic Ocean and welcoming sailors of all kinds of vessels to Portimão.

•CAFÈS•

My favourite places to have something to eat and drink

•CAFFÈ DA ROCHA•

Lovely cafè that has both inside and outside tables. Friendly staff and long menus, for both sweet and savoury snacks.

Address:

Ed. Belo Horizonte,Av. Tomás Cabreira, 8500-802 Portimão, Portugal

What I most like about it

An incredible ice cream parlour with lots of different flavours.

•LA DOLCE VITA•

35 years of Italian food history in Portimão. All my favourite Italian flavours, lots of vegetarian alternatives to choose from.

Address:

Ed Mar Azul, Av. Tomás Cabreira, 8500-802 Portimão, Portugal

What I most like about it

Heavenly Italian food and extremely friendly and polite staff.

•TERRAZZA BY LA GIOCONDA•

Terraza by La Gioconda also has both inside and outside tables. Variety of sandwiches and other food like chips and burgers.

Address:

Ed. Belo Horizonte, Av. Tomás Cabreira B, 8500-802 Portimão, Portugal

What I most like about it

Friendly staff and portion sizes that leave your tummy full in a good way.
